Walal Bethel Synod (WBS)

A vibrant and spiritually committed Synod serving the Kellem Wollega Zone of Western Ethiopia.

Establishment & Background

Established in 2022 from the Western Wollega Bethel Synod, WBS now oversees 293 congregations with over 145,000 members. Rooted in a century of mission, the Synod emphasizes local governance and holistic ministry.

Vision & Mission

Vision: Christ-centered, spiritually vibrant, resource-sustainable.
Mission: Spread the Gospel and uplift communities via education, healthcare, and social services.

Core Values

  • Faithfulness
  • Community Service
  • Integrity
  • Leadership Development
  • Inclusivity

Strategic Plan (2024–2028)

  • Evangelism & Church Growth
  • Leadership Development
  • Community Development
  • Financial Sustainability

Challenges

Regional insecurity, economic hardship, and lack of infrastructure hinder operations. Support is needed to overcome these barriers.
